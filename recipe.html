Honey Garlic Salmon

A perfectly glazed salmon fillet with a sweet and savory honey garlic sauce. Ready in under 30 minutes — as seen on tonight's episode.

Prep
10 min
Cook
20 min
Serves
4
Level
Easy

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 3 tbsp honey
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Line a baking sheet with foil.
  2. Whisk together honey, garlic, soy sauce, and lemon juice.
  3. Season salmon with salt and pepper.
  4. Sear salmon skin-side up in an oven-safe skillet for 3 minutes.
  5. Flip, pour glaze over top, then transfer to the oven.
  6. Bake 12–15 minutes. Spoon pan juices over salmon before serving.
